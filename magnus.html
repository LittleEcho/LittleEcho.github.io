WELCOME TO

THE MAGNUS ARCHIVES

In the center of London lives the Magnus Institute an institution dedicated to researching the esoteric and paranormal. within the institute lies the archives, who are tasked with recording and storing each statement given to the institute in case further investigation is necessary.

currently the archives are a mess after the recent mysterious death of the previous head archivist and now the newest archivist is appointed to clean up the cluttered archive.

Make sure to click on any tape recorders you see as you scroll.



HEAD ARCHIVIST

JONATHAN SIMS

Jon is the newly appointed head archivist of the Magnus Institute. he tends to be very serious and cold towards people at first and is very skeptical of the validity of many experiences that are reported to the institute.

Is he actually a skeptic or does he believe more than he lets on?



ARCHIVAL ASSISTANT

MARTIN BLACKWOOD

A newer addition to the archival team, Martin is warm friendly presence in the office. He’s always ready to lend a ear or fix a cup of tea, but despite his best efforts he can’t seem to get on Jon’s good side.

Could it be his good natured persona is hiding a secret?



ASSISTANT ARCHIVIST

SASHA JAMES

Sasha was originally part of the artifact storage team at the institute but when Jon was promoted to the archives, she ended up as a archival assistant. She is often the first one called to investigate the statements and her curious and inviting personality makes her a valued member of the archives.

She’s always sounded like that... right?



ARCHIVAL ASSISTANT

TIM STOKER

The self-appointed office comedian, Tim’s is always striking up a conversation with his other coworkers. Even Jon isn’t safe from listening to Tims corny jokes.

But why is such a sociable charismatic guy working in the dusty archives of the Magnus Institute?



THE STATEMENTS

The previous archivist left the archives a mess. boxes of loose statements litter the floor and papers letters and forms are covering every table and desk. Because of this Jon is recording a reading of each statement as he works his way through cleaning up the archive. statements can come is any form most commonly they are written by the statement givers and given to the archives to store but statements can also be taken orally, through video and even old letters and books.Each statement is unique and cataloged a persons experience with the strange and esoteric.
